A time-line of materials

What resources I've been using.

  • December 2016

    Humble Beginnings

    Finding a way in to the world of programming was not easy for me, I found that codeacademy provided a simple solution in a follow-a-long fashion, allowing me to slowly learn what general web-syntax(CSS & HTML) is. I completed all the courses up-to React.js and decided it was time to try to apply my new knowledge

  • January 2017

    Shot-Down

    This was my solution to coming up with new challenges for myself. Easily allowing me to reinforce the languages I learned previously. I quickly realized I don't know as much as I thought I did, codeacademy will kind of trick you into thinking you know, but you don't really know until you have to actually apply the processes outside a pretty-web-environment.

  • March 2017

    Free Code Camp

    Started to finally take an online course seriously and I'm loving it, just about 2/3rd of the way through the first of three certifications offered by these guys, and the community is very welcoming, I could not recommend them more!

  • The Future

    Into the bold unknown! I would love to make an indie-game or two, continue to build-up my arsenal of websites and apps, and hopefully find a tech-company looking for a junior developper!
