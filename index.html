A Little time-line

What resources I've been using.

  • December 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Finding a way in to the world of programming was not easy for me, I found that codeacademy provided a simple solution in a follow-a-long fashion, allowing me to slowly learn what the general web-syntax is. I completed all the courses up-to React.js and decided it was time to try to apply my new "understanding"

  • January 2017

    Shot-Down

    This was my solution to coming up with new challenges for myself. Easily allowing me to reinforce the languages I learned previously. I quickly realized I don't know as much as I thought I did, codeacademy will kind of trick you into thinking you know, but you don't really know until you have to actually apply the processes outside a pretty-web-environment.

  • March 2017

    Free Code Camp

    Started to finally take an online course seriously and I'm loving it, just about 2/3rd of the way through the first of three certifications offered by these guys, and the community is quite pleasant!
